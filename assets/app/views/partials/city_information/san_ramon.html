San Ramon at a glance

If communities experience hot streaks, San Ramon is on one for certain. Recently singled out as Americas wealthiest community per capita when wealth is viewed from a broad based perspective, it has it all. Great schools, great location, great weather, great culture, and apparently the greatest percentage of households with over 100k income. San Ramon has a powerful tax and employment base with some of the corporations who call the community home.

Location

Located in the heart of the trivalley area, straddling i680 to the north of Pleasanton and Dublin, and to the south of Danville and Alamo, just outside the shadow of Mt. Diablo.

Transit

San Ramon straddles the i680 North-South artery, and is situated midway between the i580 and i24 East-West arteries. It is serviced by the BART system, the ACE trains, and County Connection bus systems. There are numerous ride-share and carpool options including the San Ramon Transit Center.

Industry

There are many ways to judge a communitys gravitas, so to speak. One of them is to look at the sort of organizations and industries that have decided to call that community their home base. This list for San Ramon starts with energy giant Chevron. It continues in grand fashion with the multinational conglomerate AT&T, the headquarters of expanding fitness giant 24 Hour Fitness, Robert Half International, the San Ramon Regional Medical Center, and the management services focused Accenture. That is a heady list of companies to have in your home town!

Public Services

San Ramon boasts beautiful parks and open spaces, a vibrant community center, excellent food and shopping.

Schools

San Ramon has just under 80 combined public and private schools. It ranks in the 90th percentile and has earned a reputation as an exceptional scholastic district.
